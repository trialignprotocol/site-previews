About Dr. Rich

He didn't just study this path. He lived it.

Dr. David Rich once lived with bulging discs and was told he needed risky surgery, then steered toward a lifetime of medication. He refused.

Instead, he rebuilt his own health through the lifestyle-and-chiropractic approach he now teaches every patient. That lived experience is the heart of how he practices: with empathy first, and the conviction that there is almost always a better path than the operating table or the pill bottle.

For more than 25 years, Dr. Rich taught university-level allied-health professionals across the tri-state area. Many of the people treating you elsewhere may have learned from him. He's board-certified in both Connecticut and New York and has consulted with some of the largest healthcare organizations in the world. When he says there's a better way, it's earned.

Honest answers

The questions most people ask first.

Do you take my insurance?

We accept most insurance companies. The fastest way to know for sure is a quick call, bring your card and we'll check before anything begins.

Isn't this expensive?

What is a pain-free life worth? We keep plans available for almost every budget for people dedicated to taking their health back. We'll talk it through honestly at your consult.

This sounds like it'll take a long time.

Ask yourself what the next five years look like with your condition. You don't have to fix everything at once, peel back one layer, take control of one thing. If you get stuck, Dr. Rich will offer a 15-minute phone consultation to help you find the next step.
